# MaaCare
AI-powered remote maternal health monitoring platform for rural communities.

## Overview
MaaCare is a mobile health platform designed to help monitor pregnant women remotely in underserved regions. The application collects maternal health data such as blood pressure, glucose levels, weight, and symptoms. An AI-based risk prediction system analyzes the data to detect early warning signs and alert healthcare workers.

The goal is to enable early intervention and reduce maternal mortality by providing continuous monitoring and data-driven insights.

## Problem
Maternal mortality remains high in rural areas due to:
- Limited access to healthcare facilities
- Lack of regular monitoring during pregnancy
- Delayed detection of complications
- Shortage of healthcare professionals

## Solution
MaaCare provides:
- A mobile app for maternal health monitoring
- AI-based risk prediction for high-risk pregnancies
- Automated alerts to healthcare workers
- Continuous tracking of vital health indicators

## Core Features (MVP)

1. Maternal Health Data Recording
- Blood pressure
- Blood glucose
- Weight
- Symptoms

2. AI Risk Detection
- Analyze maternal vitals
- Detect high-risk pregnancy patterns
- Predict potential complications

3. Alert System
- Notify healthcare workers
- Trigger alerts for high-risk cases
- Enable early medical intervention
